Shipping Policy

CookieGram proudly ships across Canada to ensure your cookie gifts arrive fresh and on time.

Shipping Carrier

All orders are shipped using Purolator Canada.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days before shipment. During peak seasons or holidays, processing times may be slightly longer.

Delivery Times

Delivery timelines depend on the destination and selected shipping option. Estimated delivery times provided by Purolator begin once the order has been shipped.

Tracking

Once your order ships, tracking information will be provided so you can monitor its journey.

Shipping Responsibility

CookieGram is not responsible for delays caused by the carrier, weather conditions, or incorrect shipping information provided at checkout.

Freshness Commitment

We package all cookie gifts carefully to maintain freshness during transit. Please ensure someone is available to receive the package when delivered.

If you experience any issues with your shipment, please contact our support team for assistance.
