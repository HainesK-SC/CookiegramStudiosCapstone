Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you ship?

CookieGram currently ships across Canada using Purolator to ensure reliable delivery.

How long does shipping take?

Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days. Delivery timelines vary depending on your location after shipment.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order ships, you will receive tracking details so you can monitor its delivery.

What if no one is home during delivery?

We recommend ensuring someone is available to receive the package. Delivery outcomes are subject to Purolator’s policies.

Do you guarantee freshness?

Absolutely. All cookie gifts are carefully packaged to maintain freshness during transit.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders may be modified or cancelled if they have not yet been processed. Please contact support as soon as possible for assistance.

What if my package is delayed?

While we work with reliable carriers, delivery delays may occur due to weather or logistics outside of our control.

How do I contact support?

If you have any issues with your order or delivery, please reach out through our support contact listed in the footer.
